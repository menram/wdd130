History

It all started as a family vacation with my two sons. We wanted to do something fun, and while we were on vacation in Idaho, we decided to raft the Snake River. Never before had we rafted a river, but people have been doing it since ancient times. So we decided to do it, and we had a blast!

We started going to a different rapid in the US every time we had a break in school or at work. We tried kayaks, canoes, rafts, paddleboards, and tubes. Then in the year 2015 we started RMR Whitewater Rafting. We have been creating the best possible adventures for our groups ever since.
