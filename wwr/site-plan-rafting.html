Overview

Purpose

Our main purpose is to make our Whitewater Rafting options known to the public. We want to create awareness about our Whitewater Rafting offerings, increase interest in our Whitewater Rafting tours, and continually grow our Whitewater Rafting customer base. We have kayaks, canoes, rafts, paddleboards, and tubes trips.

Audience

Our target audience will be people who enjoy the outdoors, recreation, and adventure. Who are the target customers? People who love the outdoors, recreation, and adventure. This group of people love nature, have a young mindset, and are ready for action. What do they want? Destinations where to enjoy the outdoors or thrilling adventures. What are their needs that aren't being met? Time with familiy and friends, explore natural sorroundings, and exciting activities in the wild.
